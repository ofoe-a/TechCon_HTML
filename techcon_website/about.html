History

TechCon began in 2004 as a small gathering in Silicon Valley, uniting tech enthusiasts to discuss emerging trends like personal computing and mobile technology. Over the years, it evolved into a global phenomenon, showcasing breakthroughs in AI, blockchain, and sustainable tech.

Mission

At its core, TechCon is driven by a mission to ignite innovation, foster collaboration, and empower change-makers. The conference is guided by principles that emphasize inclusivity, sustainability, and cutting-edge advancements in technology.
